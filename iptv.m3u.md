## 📖 How to Use

1. Copy any of the URLs above
2. Open your IPTV player (VLC, Kodi, TiviMate, IPTV Smarters, etc)
3. Select "Add Playlist" or "Open Network Stream"
4. Paste the URL
5. Wait for channels to load

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- These are public, legal playlists
- Some streams may be region-locked (use VPN if needed)
- Try different playlists if one doesn't work
- Free streams can sometimes buffer or go offline
